Healthcare opportunity on the Arise platform

Join a structured healthcare support path by registering on Arise as an agent under our service partner. You work through Arise — not as a direct Trinitas employee for this track.

What this is

Work for a service partner on Arise

This healthcare opportunity runs on the Arise work-from-home platform. You register as an agent and join an existing service partner (not as a solo business in this flow). After you request to join, you need partner approval before you can proceed.

Service Partner ID 1241298
WhatsApp for join approval 9790113193

Critical: Name must match on PAN and Aadhaar

Your full legal name must be identical on your PAN card and Aadhaar card (spelling, order of names, and initials as printed on both documents).

  • Use the same name when you create your Arise account as printed on PAN and Aadhaar.
  • Mismatches (e.g. missing middle name, different spelling, or reverse order) often block KYC, banking, and partner approval.
  • If your documents do not match, update one through official channels before you register.

How to register on Arise

  1. Prepare documents PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, and a clear photo. Confirm the name is the same on PAN and Aadhaar.
  2. Open Arise registration Go to register.arise.com (or the official Arise agent registration page) on a desktop browser when possible.
  3. Create your account Enter your details using your legal name exactly as on PAN and Aadhaar. Use an email and mobile number you can access.
  4. Choose “Work for someone else” / join a service partner Do not set up as an independent service partner for this path. Select the option to join or work under an existing service partner.
  5. Enter Service Partner ID Use partner ID 1241298 when prompted.
  6. Request approval on WhatsApp Message 9790113193 with: full name (as on PAN/Aadhaar), Arise registered email/mobile, and that you joined partner 1241298. Wait for approval before next steps.
  7. Complete platform requirements Finish any remaining Arise profile, equipment, and program steps as shown in your Arise dashboard after approval.

Before you start

  • Name consistency — PAN and Aadhaar must show the same legal name; use that exact name on Arise.
  • Partner ID — Always use 1241298 when joining the service partner.
  • Approval — Joining is not complete until you receive confirmation via WhatsApp on 9790113193.
  • Equipment — Arise programs typically require a quiet space, stable internet, and a compliant headset/computer. Follow Arise’s current equipment checklist after login.
  • Separate from Trinitas assessment — The Trinitas preliminary assessment on the Careers page is a different track. Complete Arise steps as listed here for this healthcare partner path.
