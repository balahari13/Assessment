What this is

Work under Trinitas NextGen on Arise

This healthcare opportunity runs on the Arise work-from-home platform. You register as an Agent and join Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions as your service partner (choose “Work for someone else” / join a service partner — not as a solo business). After you request to join, Trinitas must approve you before you can proceed.

Mandatory PC & network requirements

A proper desktop or laptop PC is required. Mobile phones and tablets are not accepted for this work.

  • Minimum 8 GB RAM — systems with less memory will not meet program standards.
  • LAN (wired Ethernet) connection only — you must use a cable from your router/modem to the PC.
  • Wi‑Fi is strictly not allowed — wireless internet does not qualify for login or production work on this path.
  • Use a quiet workspace, a quality headset, and a stable power supply for full shifts.

Critical: Name must match on PAN and Aadhaar

Your full legal name must be identical on your PAN card and Aadhaar card (spelling, order of names, and initials as printed on both documents).

  • Use the same name when you create your Arise account as printed on PAN and Aadhaar.
  • Mismatches (e.g. missing middle name, different spelling, or reverse order) often block KYC, banking, and partner approval.
  • If your documents do not match, update one through official channels before you register.

How to register on Arise

  1. Prepare documents PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, and a clear photo. Confirm the name is the same on PAN and Aadhaar.
  2. Open Arise registration Go to register.arise.com (or the official Arise agent registration page) on a desktop browser when possible.
  3. Create your account Enter your details using your legal name exactly as on PAN and Aadhaar. Use an email and mobile number you can access.
  4. Choose “Work for someone else” / join a service partner Do not set up as an independent service partner. Select the option to work under an existing partner so you join as an Agent under Trinitas NextGen.
  5. Enter Trinitas Service Partner ID Enter partner ID 1241298 (Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions) when prompted.
  6. Request approval on WhatsApp Message 9790113193 with: full name (as on PAN/Aadhaar), Arise registered email/mobile, and that you joined Trinitas NextGen (Partner ID 1241298). Wait for approval before next steps.
  7. Confirm PC setup before you go live Minimum 8 GB RAM, LAN (wired) internet only — Wi‑Fi is strictly not allowed. Finish any remaining Arise profile and program steps after approval.
