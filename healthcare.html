Join as an Agent under Trinitas NextGen

Follow this path one step at a time. You work as an Agent under Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions on the Arise platform (Service Partner ID 1241298).

Step 1 of 9

Step 1

Register on Arise

Create your account on the official Arise registration site. Use a desktop browser when possible.

  • Open register.arise.com
  • Use your legal name exactly as on PAN and Aadhaar (names must match on both documents)
  • Use an email and mobile number you can access for OTP and updates
  • Keep PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details ready

Name match is mandatory

If PAN and Aadhaar names do not match, fix them through official channels before you register.

Open Arise registration

Step 2

Areas of expertise and interest

After registering, complete the profile section for areas of expertise and areas of interest on Arise. Choose options that match your skills (for example healthcare support, customer care, or related domains shown on the form).

  • Fill every required field honestly — this helps matching and approval
  • Save or continue only after expertise and interest sections are complete

Step 3

Work for Someone Else

Select Work for someone else / join an existing service partner. Do not create your own service partner business for this path.

  • You will join as an Agent under Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions
  • Service Partner name: Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions
  • Service Partner ID (enter later in this step sequence): 1241298

Step 4

Aadhaar & PAN — verify online

After “Work for someone else,” enter your Aadhaar and PAN and complete online verification on Arise.

  • Numbers and names must match your physical cards
  • Do not skip verification — partner ID comes only after this succeeds

Critical

Full legal name must be identical on PAN and Aadhaar (spelling and order of names).

Step 5

Enter Service Partner ID & request approval

Only after Aadhaar and PAN are verified online, enter Service Partner ID 1241298 for Trinitas NextGen Business Solutions.

Service Partner ID 1241298
WhatsApp for approval 9790113193

Message WhatsApp with your full name (as on PAN/Aadhaar), Arise email/mobile, and partner ID 1241298. Wait for approval before the next step.

Step 6

After approval — sign NDAs

Once your join request is approved, complete both agreements:

  • NDA (Arise) — sign the non-disclosure agreement on the Arise platform as prompted
  • Agent NDA (Trinitas) — sign the Trinitas Agent NDA when shared by the hiring team

Do not skip either document. Keep confirmation screenshots or emails for your records.

Step 7

Reach Trinitas for finalization

After both NDAs are signed, contact Trinitas for finalization of your onboarding.

  • WhatsApp: 9790113193
  • Confirm: Arise registration complete, partner ID joined, NDAs signed
  • Wait for the hiring team to confirm you are cleared for assessments

Step 8

Complete the Trinitas website assessment

After finalization from Trinitas, complete the preliminary skills assessment on this website (Careers → Attempt 1).

  • Allow about 75 minutes in a quiet place with a working microphone
  • You may pause if needed; recruitment generates an OTP to resume
  • Use stable power and your assessment device as instructed
Go to Trinitas assessment

Step 9

Take the Arise website assessment

In addition to the Trinitas assessment, complete any assessment required on the Arise website / dashboard for your program.

  • Log in to Arise and follow the assessment or certification prompts shown there
  • Finish both Trinitas and Arise assessments as advised by the hiring team

PC requirements before you go live

  • Minimum 8 GB RAM
  • LAN (wired) only — Wi‑Fi is strictly not allowed
  • Quiet workspace and quality headset
